About us

Charity Enterprises is a locally rooted poultry business based in Johannesburg, focused on supplying high-quality broiler chickens to the surrounding community. The business was built to meet the everyday demand for fresh, affordable protein while supporting local economic activity. By keeping operations close to the people it serves, Charity Enterprises ensures accessibility, reliability, and a strong connection with its customers.

At the heart of the business is a fully managed production cycle that begins with carefully sourced day-old chicks. These chicks are raised in clean, controlled environments where feeding, hydration, and overall welfare are closely monitored. This hands-on approach allows the business to maintain healthy growth rates and produce strong, well-developed birds ready for market.

Charity Enterprises places a strong emphasis on quality and consistency. Every stage of the process, from rearing to preparation, is handled with care to ensure that customers receive fresh, hygienic, and properly handled chicken. This commitment helps build trust with households, restaurants, and bulk buyers who rely on dependable supply.

Affordability is another key pillar of the business. By managing the entire value chain and reducing unnecessary middlemen, Charity Enterprises is able to offer competitive pricing without compromising on quality. This makes it a preferred option for customers who want fresh chicken at reasonable prices, especially in a market where supermarket costs can be high.

The business caters to a wide range of customers, including individual households, local food vendors, and restaurants. Whether supplying small daily needs or fulfilling large bulk orders, Charity Enterprises adapts to customer demand with flexibility and efficiency. This versatility allows it to serve both informal and formal markets effectively.

Beyond business, Charity Enterprises contributes to the local community by supporting job creation and encouraging small-scale economic growth. It represents more than just a poultry supplier—it is a dependable local enterprise committed to feeding the community, maintaining high standards, and building long-term relationships with its customers.
